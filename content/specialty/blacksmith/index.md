The Blacksmith's Bullwhip was born from a collaboration with Cornish blacksmith Reece Foster of <a href="https://www.instagram.com/saulforged/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Saul Forged</a> — a craftsman who works traditional metal on a traditional forge, the old way. It felt right to meet that energy with equal intention. The thong carries two Elder Futhark runes woven into the Thor's Hammer pattern: one for Inspiration, one for Love. The handle pays tribute to the forge itself with a Runic design, finished in rust and black with copper hardware throughout. This whip was built to last — like the work that inspired it.

Every Blacksmith's Bullwhip features 3 Elder Futhark Runes, one in the Handle and two in the Thong, one on either side of the Thor's Hammer. Select your custom runes below.
